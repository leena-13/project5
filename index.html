BLACKPINK

First music video


BOOMBAYAH Music Video by BLACKPINK

“BOOMBAYAH” was BLACKPINK’s popular debut music video, released on August 8, 2016. This MV gained 3.33 million views in the first 24 hours, and now has about 1.7 billion.

Most views music video


DDU-DU DDU-DU Music Video by BLACKPINK

As of January 2024, the most viewed music video by the K-pop group BLACKPINK on YouTube is "DDU-DU DDU-DU", with around 2.16 billion views. The song was released in June 2018, and is also the second most viewed Korean music video overall, after PSY's "Gangnam Style".

Born Pink Album


Pink Venom Video by BLACKPINK

"Pink Venom" is the very first song in their most recent album Born Pink. It was released on September 16, 2022 and had 877 million views as of today.
